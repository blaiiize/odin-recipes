Healthy and Tasty Breakfast

Description

Ingredients:

  • toast
  • turkey bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • avocado
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika

Steps:

  1. Place two pieces of toast into the air fryer
  2. Create a circle on the toast using turkey bacon slices
  3. Crack an egg into the center of each circle
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and paprika onto the eggs
  5. Set air fryer to 400F for 4 minutes
  6. Carefully take out the toast and plate with avocado slices on the side
  7. Enjoy!
