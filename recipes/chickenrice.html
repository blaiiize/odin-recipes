Healthy and Delicious Air Fryer Buffalo Chicken and Rice

Description

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast
  • rice of your choice
  • Nando's Buffalo Sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • paprika
  • ranch (optional)

Steps:

  1. Butterfly chicken breast into even halves
  2. Coat chicken breast in salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika
  3. Rub seasonings into chicken breast
  4. Place chicken breast into air fryer, with space between them
  5. Set air fryer to 400F for 8 minutes
  6. While chicken is cooking, boil water and prepare to cook rice
  7. Cook rice in the water until it's to your liking
  8. When chicken is done, flip them over in the air fryer and cook again for 7 minutes
  9. When chicken is finished cooking, plate your rice and chicken
  10. Drizzle Nando's buffalo sauce (and ranch if you want) over the dish and enjoy!
