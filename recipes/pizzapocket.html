Healthy Homemade Pizza Pocket

Description

Ingredients:

  • Xtreme Wellness large wraps
  • turkey pepperoni
  • low fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • pizza sauce
  • Hillshire Farm sliced turkey
  • garlic salt
  • ranch

Steps:

  1. Lay out wrap and place one serving of shredded mozarella on it
  2. Pour one serving of pizza sauce over the shredded mozarella
  3. Place one serving of turkey pepperoni along the sauce
  4. Place one serving of turkey slices along the sauce
  5. Sprinkle garlic salt over the wrap
  6. Roll into a burrito wrap
  7. Place in air fryer and set it for 8 minutes at 400F
  8. When the pizza pocket is finished, plate it along with ranch on the side as dipping sauce
  9. Enjoy!
